how it works

For User

1

SIGN UP AND SEARCH

Create an account on our platform, then browse through our list of skilled handymen. You can filter by location, service type, and customer reviews to find the right professional for your needs.

2

SELECT AND CONNECT

Choose a handyman based on your preferences and directly reach out to discuss the job. You can negotiate pricing, timelines, and other details without any interference from the platform.

3

GET THE JOB DONE

Once you've agreed on the terms, the handyman will complete the service. Payment and any additional arrangements are handled directly between you and the handyman.

For Handymen

1

CREATE YOUR PROFILE

Sign up on our platform and build a detailed profile showcasing your skills, experience, and areas of expertise. Add customer reviews and photos of past work to attract potential clients

2

CONNECT WITH CUSTOMERS

Customers will reach out to you directly based on your profile. You can discuss job details, negotiate pricing, and set up appointments according to your availability.

3

COMPLETE THE JOB AND GET PAID

After agreeing on the terms with the customer, complete the job to their satisfaction. Payments are made directly to you, with no platform involvement in financial transactions.
